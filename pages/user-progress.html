Developmental & Training Modules

Module Details

Progress: 0%

This training covers fry station standards and procedures, including when to drop fries, quality times, and expectations for both fries and packaging. It also reviews proper salting methods, preparing no-salt fries, fryer troubleshooting, and maintaining correct oil levels. Team members will focus on communication with Front of House, coordinating during Boosts, and completing checklists accurately and on time.

Performance Rubric

Quality Criteria

Unsatisfactory

Too much oil; unevenly salted; does not follow quality timers

Average

Shakes off oil; stirs salt but not always enough; follows quality timers in holding station but not in chute

Excellent

Lets oil drain; perfectly evenly salted; perfectly follows quality timers

Speed/Timing Criteria

Unsatisfactory

Scoops a bag in over 1 minute, 5 seconds

Average

Scoops a bag of fries in under 1 minute, 5 seconds

Excellent

Scoops a bag of fries in under 50 seconds

Communication Criteria

Unsatisfactory

Refuses to call for boosts; FOH unable to park because of lack of communicating holds; does not parrot what FOH communicates to kitchen

Average

Communicates with leader and FOH but with some inconsistencies, calls for boost when pressure is called but not proactively

Excellent

Always on top of communicating to leader and FOH, calls for boost proactively to avoid any holds; always parrots what FOH calls to kitchen
